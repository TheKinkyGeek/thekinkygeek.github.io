# Formerly once

I don't remember the entire development history. I'd like to show the things I do remember here.

## Remotes Generation 1

Initially, I had three buttons on each side to configure and control the session. The red blocks were the emergency stop buttons. I wanted to make sure I could operate them even while wearing very thick latex gloves.
